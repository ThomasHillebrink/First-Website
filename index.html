Päiväkirja 13.05.2019

Today I started at Digitalents for my first day. Naturally, I started the day with a mixture of nerves and excitement so common when starting a new adventure. Further increased with the knowledge that the two familiar faces I knew would not be present in the morning I would start.

Upon arriving on the location I met Elli, who then guided me to the central hall where we would be having breakfast. Afterwards I attended a short presentation giving by Krishna which I didn’t fully understand, but the gist of it was clear to me. After a short introductory round I spoke with Krishna for a while to determine my study path. We determined my aspirations, motivations and challenges, and after a quick lunch break I got the work on the first assignments I was given.

The Canva assignment asked me to make a photo collage summarizing of why I came here, and why I want to continue. After giving a good thought, boiling it down to the essence of why I do what I do, I decided that I could accurately summarize it to the word: Love. Love for another person is what brought me here. Love for where I ended up is why I stayed. And Love for myself is why I keep on going.

With Finishing the Canva assignment, I moved on to going over the basics of HTML and CSS again, this time on ILoveCoding. My first impressions on the method are very positive. The videos are well structured, and give enough space and opportunity to play around independently, while also giving examples of different resources and programs available. Today I got to lesson 17 out of 71, and I aspire to finish this course before the end of the week.

-Thomas
